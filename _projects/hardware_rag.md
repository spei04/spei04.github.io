**Computer Systems Architecture Final Project**

Modeled hardware efficiency of RAG inference pipelines across LPDDR4/5X/6 memory technologies, quantifying DRAM, SRAM, and MAC energy/latency tradeoffs using Accelforge with GQA for optimization.

- Analyzed the impact of Flash Attention fusion mapping on end-to-end RAG latency and energy
- Evaluated four RAG pipeline topologies (sequential, conditional, branching, loop) and three memory generations, producing component-level energy and latency breakdowns
