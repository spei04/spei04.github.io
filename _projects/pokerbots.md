**MIT Pokerbots Competition** — Jan 2025

Built a bot to compete in a modified version of poker with bounty cards (winning hands containing the bounty card receive an increased reward).

- Created a **bandit bot** that adapts its strategy to each opponent and deploys the best-performing strategy dynamically
- Implemented counterfactual regret minimization (CFR) and reinforcement learning for strategy optimization
- Designed to detect opponent tendencies and exploit weaknesses in real-time
