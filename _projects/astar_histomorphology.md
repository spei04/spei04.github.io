**A\*STAR Bioinformatics Institute** — May 2024 – Aug 2024 · Advised by Dr. Lau Mai Chan

Implemented an unsupervised model for feature extraction and tissue clustering for histomorphology in H&E-stained liver-cancer tissue images.

- Wrote **5,000+ lines of Python** for model training, evaluation, and data visualization
- Conducted literature reviews of state-of-the-art deep-learning models, clustering methods, and self-supervised encoders
- Optimized code for opening high-resolution whole-slide images and overlaid predicted cluster masks to analyze accuracy
