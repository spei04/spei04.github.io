**MIT YC Program** — Jan 2026 – Feb 2026  
*16 out of 100 teams from MIT selected, $5K funding*

Explored three enterprise AI products through rapid prototyping, customer discovery, and weekly demos:

- **Procurement optimization tool** — price optimization with AI-powered suggestions for increasing workplace efficiency
- **Field technician assistant** — AI-powered phone-based assistant enabling field technicians to quickly identify industrial parts from unindexed PDFs and public data, using FastAPI, React, Supabase, Vapi (voice API), FireCrawl (web scraping), and Reducto (PDF parsing)
- **Contract automation tool** — automation for contract execution and deal closing, using FastAPI + React, Supabase, Vapi web call, Google Auth, GPT API, Railway (backend), and Vercel (frontend)

Conducted direct customer calls and industry trend research, with weekly demos and group brainstorming sessions.
