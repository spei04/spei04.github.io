**Apart Hackathon & AgentBeats Hackathon** — Dec 2025 – Present

Built **ID-Bench**, a pipeline to evaluate AI agent safety in simulated financial trading scenarios.

- Developed a dual-pipe evaluation architecture capturing both public actions and internal "confessions," producing metrics including violation rate, deception gap, and safety-adjusted alpha
- Automated scenario-driven testing and leaderboard aggregation using open-source embeddings for semantic comparison of agent reasoning
- Benchmarks AI agent compliance, deception, and decision-making quality under adversarial trading conditions
