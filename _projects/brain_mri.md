**Computer Vision Final Project** — April–May 2025

Introduced a lightweight model to produce realistic brain MRI images with glioma tumors using a fine-tuned stable diffusion model.

- Fine-tuned a stable diffusion model (CLIP, VAE, U-Net) using brain MRI target/source/text triples
- Used Gaussian blur, checkerboard, and edge maps as source conditioning images
- Analyzed qualitative and quantitative metrics (FID, SSIM) of synthesized images to evaluate realism and clinical fidelity
