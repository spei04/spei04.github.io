**MIT CSAIL Vision Lab** — Sep 2025 – May 2026 · Advised by Sara Beery

Researched and implemented state-of-the-art approaches to address distribution shift by finetuning diffusion models.

- Developed **generative AI pipelines** (Qwen Edit and Flux) and **finetuned diffusion models** (DreamBooth) to balance species classification datasets for data augmentation; explored curriculum learning and MLLM-based filtering to improve synthetic-data usability
- Trained classification models on augmented datasets to establish baseline accuracies and designed rigorous evaluation methods
- Presented a [poster](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1js7wec4vULfK7B_Sva4B3KPDtzX6T7BW/view?usp=sharing) to **OpenAI sponsors**, my lab group, and the **MIT Generative AI Impact Consortium**
