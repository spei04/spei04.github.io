**MIT Media Lab — Fluid Interfaces Lab** — Feb 2024 – May 2026 · Advised by Nataliya Kosmyna

Research on biosignal processing (EEG, EMG, EOG), human-computer interaction, and adaptive AI systems.

- **First author** — *Frontiers in Human Neuroscience* & *MIT Undergraduate Research Journal*: surface-EMG analysis of speech motor patterns in minimally verbal ASD adults
- **Co-author** — *IEEE BSN 2024*: machine-learning prediction of eye movement from EOG and EEG signal data
- **Co-author** — *ACM IMWUT*: emotion regulation via heartbeat recognition and valence/arousal disturbance, with designed experiments and statistical tests
- **First author** — multimodal (EOG/EEG/EMG) feedback study in minimally verbal ASD individuals, optimizing classification accuracy with ML models
- Led a multimodal ML study; mentored and onboarded new students and wrote 2,000+ lines of Python for analysis
